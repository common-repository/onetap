This embedded UX feature allows an end user to sign in directly when a Google iframe is embedded into your web pages, enabling a frictionless user sign-in experience. The Automatic sign-in option allows users to sign in without having to go through the traditional login process, making it more convenient and user-friendly. This feature eliminates the need for users to remember multiple usernames and passwords, as they can simply use their existing Google account to sign in. This can lead to increased conversion rates, as users are more likely to complete a purchase or take other desired actions when the login process is quick and easy. It also offers an added layer of security, as it ensures that only the user's verified Google account is used for login.
